About Leucocyte Classification

Leucocyte classification using deep learning is a powerful approach to identifying different types of white blood cells in microscopic images.Accurate classification of leucocytes is crucial for diagnosing infections, immune disorders, and blood-related diseases such as leukemia.

Traditional methods are often time-consuming and prone to human error, while deep learning offers automation and enhanced precision. Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) are commonly used due to their ability to extract complex features from image data.

These models can learn patterns and differences between cell types such as neutrophils, lymphocytes, monocytes, eosinophils, and basophils. Overall, deep learning significantly improves diagnostic efficiency and supports clinical decision-making in hematology.
